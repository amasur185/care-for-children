My Mission

Why I'm Raising Funds

I believe education is a universal right — and that one classroom can break a cycle of poverty that has lasted generations.

Care For Children gives free, value-based education to more than 120,000 underprivileged children across 1,356 schools in rural, tribal, and slum communities throughout India. These are children who would otherwise have no access to school at all. The results speak for themselves: 100% academic results, 90%+ attendance, and zero dropouts.

As someone who tutors students and has spent 70+ hours volunteering in my community, I've seen how much of a difference a little support and encouragement can make. I want to extend that impact across the world — to kids who simply need a chance. That's why I'm dedicating my time and energy to this cause.
