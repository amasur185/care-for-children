About Me

I'm a highly motivated student who loves math and technology, and I have a deep passion for helping kids in need. I enjoy learning how things work, solving problems, and building new skills. As a consistent honor-roll student, I'm always excited for new opportunities to grow and give back.

Outside the classroom, I've trained in Bharatanatyam (Indian classical dance) for 10 years and tutor middle and high school students in science and math. Helping others understand and succeed is something I'm genuinely passionate about.

Giving back is a big part of who I am. I've logged over 100 volunteer hours in my community, packing food for the homeless, supporting mind and breathwork events, cleaning up with I Love A Clean San Diego, and volunteering with Feeding America. This fundraiser is my way of extending that same spirit of service to children who need it most.

My Mission

Why I'm Raising Funds

I believe education is a universal right, and that one classroom can break a cycle of poverty that has lasted generations.

Care For Children gives free, value-based education to more than 120,000 underprivileged children across 1,356 schools in rural, tribal, and slum communities throughout India. These are children who would otherwise have no access to school at all. The results speak for themselves: 100% academic results, 90%+ attendance, and zero dropouts.

As someone who tutors students and has spent 100+ hours volunteering in my community, I've seen how much of a difference a little support and encouragement can make. I want to extend that impact across the world, to kids who simply need a chance. That's why I'm dedicating my time and energy to this cause.
